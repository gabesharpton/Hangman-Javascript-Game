Welcome to Hangman

In this game you have a random word selected for you from a library of words I created.
You get a total of 6 wrong guesses before you completed the death of stick man. If you select the same letter twice you lose a guess. Do you think you know enough words to not doom Mr. Stick to the gallows?
Well lets find out!!
